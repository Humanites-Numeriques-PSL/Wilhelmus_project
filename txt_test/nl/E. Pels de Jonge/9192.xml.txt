 IEn Vryer uyt vrye Teegh, en aen sen sye Droech hy sijn Deeghen mee. Ierst ging hy by Haesje, By Luytje en Claesje, Maer hy en houde gien stee. By Wybrecht en Trijntje, By Duysje en Stijntje, By Swaentje, Ursel, en Griet, By Brechje, by Baertje, By Aeltje, by Claertje, En vond' hy sijn gaedingh niet. By Wayn, by Annetje, By Lijs, by Tannetje, By Eva, by Hillegont, By Beeltie, by Lijntje, Noch by Jacomijntje, Hy oock sen slach niet en vont. By Truy, by Sannetje, Marry en Jannetje, By Krelisje noch by Tiet, By Raeghel, by Sijtje, By Barbel, by Fijtje, Hy mee een blauwe Scheen stiet. By Joosje, by Kniertje, By Dieuwer, by Giertje, By Lobbetje, en by Saer, By Machtel, by Lutje, By Hayltje, by Jutje, En was het heylick niet claer. By Elsje, by Aefje, By Cely, by Baesje, En by Maynsje al te met, By Niesje, by Jopje, Pietertje, en Sopje, En Ariaentje was sen tret. By Jaepje, by Soetje, By Hester, by Oetje, By Lenoor, en Magdaleen, By Geesje, by Baycke, By Enghel, by Maycke, En Ysje was hy ghemeen. By Heleen, by Fransje, By Neel, by Constansje, BY Isebeel, by Ding'na, By Ytje, by Teuntje, By Vroutje, by Pleuntje, Quam hy praten t'savents spae. Princesse dit maetje, Dit layde een praetje Op ien avent by heur al, Dit waren sy even, Als ick hebeschreven, Drie vijf-en-twintich is 'tal.
